opinion on facebook,many of you who read this might agree or disagree and it
will be my pleasure if you correct me
So my point here is, i was going through videos on youtube on various debates regarding various
topics as to why pakistan refuses to talk to india
and about intolerance and about about anti national slogans ,one thing i learnt
from the Pakistani guests is that they are much more patriotic to their country
than we will ever be. I agree that their leaders make funny remarks but i have
never seen any Pakistani news anchor or guests go against their leader,they
support the idea of leadership and they don't question on the governance of
their leadership which we often do.
And i just thought why do people choose any government and what role does
opposition play, as i have read in my social science classes that the opposition
is to remark on mistakes of the government an it is the people of india who
choose them,so in my suggestion let the
government do their work and let the opposition do theirs ,why the hell do we poke our nose ,i understand about
right to speech and expression but if you go on making remarks and opposing
government then the whole idea of opposition is failure
But most important thing i want to say is that everyone has flaws but there are
qualities too and one thing i wanna learn from Pakistanis is that they support their
government no matter what...i don't know about their home affairs and
seriously no one cares but it is not same for India, the whole world is looking
at us because we are doing blunders so we have to keep that in mind and set
example of what people of india are
The last thing i wanna say is that we should create a platform where everyone
can voice their opinion but doing rallies and stuff are not helping but creating
hatred and division in society
